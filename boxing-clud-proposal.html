Proposal for Boxing Club Boat Trip

Introduction

Maintaining a healthy work-life balance is essential for both physical and mental well-being. As part of our ongoing efforts to foster team camaraderie and promote relaxation outside the gym, we propose a day-long boat trip. This will offer a fantastic opportunity for everyone to unwind, explore new locations, and enjoy time together in a relaxed setting, away from the stresses of daily routines.

Details of the Trip

  • Cost per Person: GMD 1,200
    The cost includes food and visits to several beautiful locations during the trip.
  • Time: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
    The boat owner has recommended these times to ensure we can enjoy a full day of activities and relaxation.
  • Location: We will visit various picturesque spots along the journey, allowing us to experience new environments, enjoy nature, and spend quality time together.

Proposed Benefits

  1. Physical and Mental Well-being: This boat trip provides a refreshing break from the rigors of training, offering an opportunity to recharge mentally and physically.
  2. Team Bonding: Spending time together outside the gym fosters stronger relationships within the club, which translates to better teamwork during training.
  3. Work-Life Balance: As many of us juggle work, training, and personal commitments, this event encourages a healthy balance, reminding us of the importance of relaxation.
  4. Exploration: The trip will allow us to explore new locations, contributing to our sense of adventure and appreciation for the outdoors.

Logistics

  • Date: To be determined based on everyone's availability. We will circulate a survey to find a date that works best for the majority.
  • Departure Time: 10:00 a.m.
  • Return Time: 5:00 p.m.
    The suggested timing ensures a full day of exploration, bonding, and relaxation.
  • Food: Meals are included in the price, ensuring everyone is well-nourished throughout the day.
  • Group Activities: Team-building exercises
